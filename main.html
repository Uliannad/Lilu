About Lilu

Lilu began as a heartfelt idea between two best friends — Lisa and Ulia. We’ve always shared a love for flowers, beautiful details, and creating moments that matter. One day, over coffee and endless dreams, we decided to turn that love into something real.

The name Lilu comes from the combination of our names — a symbol of our friendship and shared vision. From the very beginning, we built everything ourselves: picking flowers at dawn, arranging our first bouquets late into the night, wrapping each order with laughter and care.

Every bouquet we create is more than just flowers — it's a message, a mood, a memory. We believe that flowers can speak when words fall short, and every petal carries meaning.

Through ups and downs, busy seasons and quiet mornings, we’ve stood side by side — two friends with a passion, growing something beautiful together.

Our studio is our second home, and every arrangement that leaves it carries a piece of our heart — and hopefully, a bit of magic.

To be continued…
